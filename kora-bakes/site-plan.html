Content

Home page

The home page will contain a hero image, products and information section, and about us section. The goal of the home page is to encourage users to view the website even more and gain more clients in the process. It will display several products in the hero section to allow users and potential clients to view featured products.

Images for the Home page

Products Page

This page will contain a lot of interactivity for the web app to engage potential clients more to purchase orders. The product page will contain all of the information from different products to allow user to get to know the product even more. They will also have the option to request an order and be directed to the contact us/order page.

Images for the Page 2

Contact Us

This page will contain a form to get orders and a section for showing feedbacks from other clients. This is the page where users will be redirected from the products page to order different products that they want. In the back end, it will notify us when an order is requested.
